About ourselves

We are a group of students who are approaching the world of photography, linked by the curiosity of exploring and capture everything around us by using our cameras. This website is the product of our commitment and creativity, and is developed as a part of the photography course organised by our school. In this site we will show our works, experiences and knowledge acquired through this course. Every photo represents a piece of our learning path and artistic growth. We hope our images will inspire you as much as we were inspired creating them.

Welcome to our photographic journey!


How the project began

This project is a product of the idea and photographic passion of a teacher of Italian and history of our school: Patrizia Gesini. Because of her passion for photography and her previous experience, she wanted to pass on her passion to us thanks to this course: "Contrasts in Daily Life".

The course was financed by the PNRR (National Recovery and Resilience Plan) and placed inside the school project “SintonizziAMOci: alta "FREQUENZA" al Marconi M4C1I1.4-2022-981-P-21407”, which aims at reducing the school dropout and to encourage greater participation and involvement of all students of the school. There has been of a total of approximately 25 students distributed between the various sections of the classes of the third, fourth and fifth year. Even if many participants of the course are students of Teacher Gesini, the project has had great partecipation from other students of the school, demonstrating a collective interest in the topics covered.
